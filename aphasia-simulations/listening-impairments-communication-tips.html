Listening Impairments Communication Tips

Try these basic strategies to help with listening comprehension:

  • Speak slowly but naturally, with pauses between phrases
  • Repeat key words or entire phrases
  • Write down key words
  • Use gestures when possible while talking
  • Paraphrase and repeat phrases as needed
